A life club for the youths mentored by the founder of DeArosto institute

Achievers’ Club is a life club for the youths mentored by the founder of DeArosto institute, Sir Arosto Ben.
The club aims at;

  • Helping the youths live a life that is regrets-free
  • Training life skills
  • helping students utilize their academic potential optimally.

The club is divided into three sections known as “Tiers”. These are Juniors Tier, Tertiary tier, and Vicenarian Tier. Each Tier is headed by a Tier Lead Mentor, TLM, who works closely with the Tier Mentors to ensure the objectives of the club are achieved.
In School, the club is headed by the School Mentor, who is fully in charge of the club mentors in the school and gives regular feedback on the progress of the students.
They also act on behalf of the TLM.

The club has two types of membership renewable yearly;
  • Individual Member Memberships.
  • School Membership.
To join the club, as an individual, one must meet the following requirements;
  • Be a high Schooler in Currently in Form 2 or 1.
  • Duly filled application form.
  • An introduction Letter from school or School identity card.
  • Ksh 500 Application fees.
What the club receives after joining;
  • An Introduction/Acceptance letter.
  • Academic Journal designed and authored by the club Lead mentor, Sir. Arosto Ben.
  • Club Badge.
  • Virtual Mentorship session during school holidays.
  • Free Guidance and Counselling.
  • Free Professional Counselling.
To register an Institution as a member.
  • Be a registered education Institution.
  • Have at least twenty students registered as members.
  • Duly filled application form.
  • Ksh 5000 registration fee.
What a registered institution receive
  • A registration Certificate.
  • Club Merchandise Products.
  • Free in-person Motivation speeches (One per term)
  • Virtual Prefect Training.
  • Subsidized fee on services offered by DeArosto Institute.
